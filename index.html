Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Taizo Testing.

Scope and Overview:
This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") outlines the commitments and services provided by Taizo.in to Taizo Testing ("Client") for the recruitment of candidates in the manufacturing industry.
1.Turnaround Time for Suitable Candidates:
Taizo.in commits to presenting suitable candidates fulfilling Taizo Testing's job requirements within 5 days from the date of request submission or plan purchase.
2.Dedicated Account Management:
A dedicated Account Manager will ensure seamless coordination and communication between Taizo.in and Taizo Testing throughout the recruitment process.
3.Tailored Recruitment Services:
A committed team with significant expertise in mechanical and manufacturing will assist in sourcing, screening, and shortlisting candidates specifically tailored to meet Taizo Testing's hiring needs.
4.Candidate Interview Line-Up:
Taizo.in will arrange and coordinate interviews for the shortlisted candidates, facilitating the selection process until Taizo Testing's hiring needs are fulfilled.
5.Position Closure:
Taizo.in will work diligently to assist Taizo Testing in successfully closing the positions, this includes follow-up with candidates, managing offer negotiations, and ensuring a smooth transition to their new roles.
